1. Opening app:
	- Download jar
	- Run jar using following command:
		java -jar <jar-name>
	  Or open the jar by double clicking it
2. Creating User:
	- Home menu will pop up after running the jar. Click on the <> button.
	- The screen will prompt you for your username, password, name, age, weight
	- Once you have entered those values click on the <> button
	- NOTE: You must enter a unique username or else your user will be rejected

3. Create New Workout:
	- On the Main Menu click the <new workout> button
	- The screen will prompt you for the workout name, date, duration (in minutes), 
	  and type (choose from the given list of workouts)
	- To create the new workout click the <> button, this will add the entry to the database
	- The app will take you to the results page showing all your workouts ordered by date

4. View Workouts:
	- On the Main Menu click the <view workout> button
	- The screen will prompt you for your query, click the <> button to go to the results page
	- You will be taken to the results page to view the workouts.
	  To go back to the main menu click the <back> button
